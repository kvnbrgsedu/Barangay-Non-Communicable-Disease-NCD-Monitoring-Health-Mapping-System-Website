What This System Does

A dedicated platform for barangay leaders, health workers, and planners to monitor non-communicable diseases and respond effectively.

Real-Time Monitoring

Keep track of NCD cases per household and zone, updated by accredited barangay health workers.

Data Visualization

Interactive maps and dashboards show disease clusters, case trends, and high-risk areas at a glance.

Community Health Awareness

Provide residents and leaders with clear, accessible information on NCDs and prevention strategies.

Why Health Mapping Matters

Health mapping transforms raw data into clear visuals that help stakeholders see where support is most needed.

  • Identify Disease Clusters – visualize areas with concentrated NCD cases.
  • Support Faster Response – enable barangay officials to mobilize resources quickly.
  • Guide Health Planning – inform targeted programs, education, and outreach activities.
